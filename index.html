What we do

To be a valuable asset for our community by preparing our children to be highly skilled employees and educated citizens capable of making informed decisions about global and regional issues and produce college, life and career-ready students who are equipped to meet current and future societal challenges

Goal

Our goal is to bridge the educational achievement gap for underserved children and youth and diversify participation in STEM studies to afford Zanzinbari children the best opportunity to compete for jobs in the global economy of the future.
